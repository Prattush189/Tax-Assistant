## HRA Exemption (Old Regime Only)
Exempt = Least of:
1. Actual HRA received
2. Rent paid – 10% of basic salary
3. 50% of basic (metro: Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata, Chennai) / 40% (non-metro)

Salary = Basic + DA + Commission (% of turnover). Rent receipts needed if annual rent > 1L. Landlord PAN needed if > 1L/year.

## Capital Gains Tax — FY 2025-26

| Asset | STCG Rate | Holding for LTCG | LTCG Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Listed equity / equity MF | 20% | >= 12 months | 12.5% | LTCG exempt up to 1.25L/year (Sec 112A) |
| Debt MF / MLDs | Slab rate | N/A | N/A | Always slab rate (no LTCG benefit since FY 2023-24) |
| Real estate | Slab rate | >= 24 months | 12.5% (no indexation) | Pre-Jul 2024 buys: option of 20% with indexation |
| Gold / Gold MF / ETF | Slab rate | >= 24 months | 12.5% | No indexation from FY 2024-25 |
| Unlisted shares | Slab rate | >= 24 months | 12.5% | No indexation |
| Sovereign Gold Bond | Exempt | >= 8 years (maturity) | Exempt on maturity | LTCG taxable if sold before maturity |

**Sec 54:** LTCG on residential property exempt if reinvested in new house within 2 years (purchase) / 3 years (construction). Max 10Cr.
**Sec 54EC:** LTCG on land/building exempt up to 50L if invested in specified bonds (NHAI/REC) within 6 months. 5-year lock-in.
**Sec 54F:** LTCG on any asset (non-house) exempt if net consideration invested in new house.

## GST Rate Structure (Post Sep 2025 Reform)

| Rate | Category | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| 0% | Essentials | Fresh fruits, vegetables, milk, bread, eggs, cereals, education, healthcare |
| 5% | Daily needs | Packaged food, butter, cheese, fruit juice, coal, footwear (<1000), transport |
| 18% | Standard | Most goods & services: AC, TV, motorbikes, IT services, restaurants (non-AC), financial services |
| 40% | Demerit/Sin | Tobacco, pan masala, aerated beverages |
| 3% | Special | Gold, silver, platinum |

**Composition scheme:** Turnover <= 1.5Cr (goods) / 50L (services), flat 1-6% tax.
**GST registration threshold:** 40L (goods) / 20L (services), 20L/10L for special category states.
**Input Tax Credit (ITC):** Available to registered businesses on business purchases. Reverse charge on certain services.

## Key Budget 2025 Changes (FY 2025-26 vs FY 2024-25)
1. New regime basic exemption: 3L -> 4L
2. New regime restructured to 7 slabs (wider bands)
3. 87A rebate: 25,000 (<=7L) -> 60,000 (<=12L)
4. Effective zero-tax: 7L -> 12L (12.75L salaried)
5. TDS on rent (194I): 2.4L/year -> 6L/year (50K/month)
6. TDS on senior citizen interest (194A): 50K -> 1L
7. No changes to old regime slabs, capital gains, or deduction limits
8. Updated return filing deadline: 31 July (non-audit), 31 Oct (audit)
