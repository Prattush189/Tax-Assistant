**A. Create `server/data/reference-data.txt`** with `======` delimited sections matching the comparison.txt pattern. Each section's first line after the delimiter MUST follow the `N. SECTION TITLE` pattern for the splitter's label extraction regex to work.

The file structure must be:

1. **Preamble** (before first `======`): Title "TAX REFERENCE GUIDE -- INDIA FY 2025-26" with brief intro explaining the guide covers CII, due dates, and ITR forms.

2. **Section 1: COST INFLATION INDEX (CII) TABLE** (REF-01)
   - Base year explanation: FY 2001-02 = 100
   - Indexation formula: Indexed Cost = Original Cost x (CII of Sale Year / CII of Purchase Year)
   - When indexation applies (long-term capital assets held > 24 months; 36 months for immovable property acquired before certain date)
   - Budget 2024 note: For immovable property acquired BEFORE 23 July 2024 and sold AFTER that date, taxpayer may choose either (a) 20% LTCG with indexation OR (b) 12.5% LTCG without indexation -- whichever gives lower tax
   - Full CII table: all 25 rows from FY 2001-02 (100) through FY 2025-26 (376)
   - Use EXACT values from RESEARCH.md verified data (CBDT Notification No. 70/2025)
   - Explicit note: "FY 2026-27: NOT YET NOTIFIED by CBDT (as of April 2026)"
   - Do NOT include FY 2026-27 value (locked user decision)

3. **Section 2: DUE DATES -- INCOME TAX** (REF-02 partial)
   - Advance tax quarterly dates: Q1 15 June, Q2 15 September, Q3 15 December, Q4 15 March with cumulative percentages (15%, 45%, 75%, 100%)
   - Senior citizen exemption note (60+ with no business income)
   - ITR filing deadlines: non-audit individuals/HUF 31 July 2026, non-audit business 31 August 2026, audit cases 31 October 2026, TP cases 30 November 2026, belated 31 December 2026 (with late fee note), revised 31 March 2027
   - TDS return deadlines (24Q/26Q): Q1 31 July, Q2 31 October, Q3 31 January, Q4 31 May
   - Note AY/Tax Year mapping: "FY 2025-26 = Tax Year 2025-26 = AY 2026-27"

4. **Section 3: DUE DATES -- GST RETURNS** (REF-02 partial)
   - GSTR-1: monthly filers (>5 Cr turnover) 11th of following month; quarterly/QRMP filers (<=5 Cr) 13th of month after quarter
   - GSTR-3B: monthly filers 20th of following month; QRMP filers 22nd or 24th of month after quarter (state-dependent)
   - GSTR-9 (Annual): 31 December 2026
   - Note the turnover threshold that determines monthly vs quarterly filing

5. **Section 4: DUE DATES -- CHRONOLOGICAL CALENDAR (FY 2025-26)** (REF-02 partial)
   - Month-by-month listing from April 2025 through March 2027 (covering the full filing cycle)
   - Combine IT, TDS, and GST deadlines in chronological order
   - Each entry: date, what is due, who it applies to

6. **Section 5: ITR FORM SELECTION MATRIX** (REF-03)
   - Cover all 7 forms: ITR-1 (Sahaj), ITR-2, ITR-3, ITR-4 (Sugam), ITR-5, ITR-6, ITR-7
   - For each form: who files, key eligibility conditions, income thresholds

   - ITR-4 MUST include the AY 2026-27 change: up to 2 house properties now allowed
   - Include both old and new regime eligibility notes where relevant
   - Cover all assessee types: individuals, HUFs, firms, LLPs, companies, trusts, AOPs

Use EXACT data values from 09-RESEARCH.md "Verified Reference Data" section. Do not estimate or approximate any CII value, date, or threshold.
