Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

This policy explains our position on refunds and how to cancel a subscription to Smartbiz AI ("the Service"). It applies to every paid plan offered on ai.smartbizin.com and forms part of our Terms of Service.

All payments are final. We do not offer refunds.
Every user receives a 30-day free trial with full feature access so you can evaluate the Service in detail before committing. Once you upgrade to a paid plan, no refunds will be issued — partial or full — for any unused portion of your subscription.

1. The 30-day free trial

When you sign up, your account is automatically placed on a 30-day free trial. During this period you have access to the Service's standard features so you can verify that the Service meets your needs. We strongly encourage you to use the trial period to evaluate:

  • Whether the AI outputs meet the quality standard you require for your practice
  • Whether the workflows fit your typical client mix and document formats
  • Whether the platform's available bank statement, ledger, and ITR formats cover your needs
  • How the Service integrates with your existing tools

The trial requires no payment information and no commitment. If the Service does not meet your needs, simply do not upgrade — your account will move to a read-only state after the trial ends, with no charge.

2. No refunds after payment

Because we provide a substantive evaluation window before charging, paid subscriptions are non-refundable, including but not limited to the following situations:

  • You change your mind after paying
  • You did not use the Service during a paid billing period
  • You used only a fraction of the features included in your plan
  • You downgrade or close your account mid-period
  • Your business circumstances change
  • An upstream provider (Google Gemini, Razorpay) experiences a temporary outage that does not result in extended Service downtime

3. Cancelling auto-renewal

You may cancel your subscription's auto-renewal at any time from the Plan page inside the Service, or by emailing prattush.jain@gmail.com.

  • Cancellation stops future charges from Razorpay.
  • Your access continues until the end of the period you have already paid for.
  • No partial refund is issued for the unused portion.
  • You may continue to use the Service normally until the period ends, after which the account moves to a read-only state.

4. Limited exceptions

We may, at our sole discretion, issue a refund in these narrow situations:

  • Duplicate charge — Razorpay processes your payment twice for the same period due to a payment-gateway error. Contact us within 7 days with the Razorpay transaction IDs and we will reverse the duplicate.
  • Unauthorised charge — your account was charged after an unauthorised third party gained access. Report immediately with evidence; we will investigate and act in accordance with the Privacy Policy and applicable law.
  • Extended Service outage caused by our infrastructure (not by upstream providers or events beyond our reasonable control) that exceeds 72 continuous hours and materially prevents use. We will issue prorated service credits, applied to your next billing period — not a cash refund.

These exceptions are not entitlements. Each request is evaluated on its facts. Decisions are final.

5. How to contact us about a refund request

Email prattush.jain@gmail.com with the subject "Refund Request" and include:

  • The registered email on your account
  • The Razorpay transaction ID(s)
  • A clear description of the issue and any relevant screenshots

We respond to refund-related requests within 7 business days. If you are pursuing a refund under one of the limited exceptions above, please raise the request within 30 days of the disputed charge.

6. Chargebacks

Please contact us before raising a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. Most issues can be resolved more quickly directly. We reserve the right to suspend accounts that initiate chargebacks without first attempting to resolve the matter with us in good faith.

7. Changes to this policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified in advance. The policy in effect at the time of payment governs that payment.
