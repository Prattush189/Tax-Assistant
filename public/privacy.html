Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Smartbiz AI ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use our AI-assisted tax and accounting application available at ai.smartbizin.com ("the Service"). We are committed to compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) of India.

1. Who we are

Smartbiz AI is operated by the team behind the Service. For privacy-related inquiries, including exercising any of your rights under the DPDP Act, contact:

  • Email: support@smartbizindia.com
  • Grievance Officer: contact details available on request via the email above

3. Purposes for which we use your data

  • Provide the Service — accept your inputs and return AI-generated outputs (tax calculations, draft notices, ledger analysis, etc.)
  • Maintain your account, enforce session limits, and prevent unauthorised access
  • Process payments and issue invoices in compliance with Indian tax law
  • Improve the Service through aggregated, non-identifying analytics
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations (GST, IT Act, DPDP Act, court orders)

We process this data under the legal bases of consent (account creation, optional features) and legitimate uses as defined in the DPDP Act, including performance of the service you requested and prevention of fraud.

4. Third parties we share data with

We share the minimum data necessary with the following processors, each bound by contractual confidentiality and security obligations:

  • Razorpay — payment processor. Razorpay receives your billing details and payment information directly; we receive only the transaction reference and outcome.
  • Email service — used to send OTP codes, password reset links, and account notifications.
  • Cloud hosting infrastructure — our servers and database are hosted in data centres within India; backups are encrypted.

We do not sell your personal data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose beyond providing the Service.

5. International data transfers

Some of the services and infrastructure we rely on may process limited personal data outside India. Such transfers comply with Section 16 of the DPDP Act and are restricted to jurisdictions not designated as restricted by the Central Government.

6. Data retention

  • Account data — retained for as long as your account is active, plus 90 days after closure for audit and chargeback windows.
  • Content (bank statements, ITR drafts, etc.) — retained while your account is active; you may delete individual items at any time via the application.
  • Billing records — retained for 8 years to comply with the Income Tax Act and GST Act audit requirements.
  • Session metadata — automatically pruned when a session is revoked or after 30 days of inactivity.
  • Logs — typically retained for 90 days unless investigating a security incident.

7. Your rights under the DPDP Act

As a Data Principal, you have the right to:

  • Access a summary of the personal data we process about you
  • Correct, complete, update, or erase your personal data
  • Withdraw consent previously granted (this may limit your ability to use the Service)
  • Nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity
  • Lodge a grievance with our Grievance Officer (response within 30 days)
  • Appeal to the Data Protection Board of India if your grievance is not resolved

To exercise any right, email support@smartbizindia.com with the subject "DPDP Request". We will verify your identity and respond within a reasonable period, not exceeding the statutory limit.

8. Security

  • Passwords are stored hashed with bcrypt; we never see your plaintext password.
  • All connections to the Service use HTTPS / TLS.
  • Session tokens are stored server-side and can be individually revoked from Settings → Active Sessions.
  • Database backups are encrypted at rest.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel and logged.

9. Children

The Service is intended for users aged 18 years or above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Cookies and local storage

We use cookies and browser localStorage for essential functionality only — to keep you signed in, remember your theme preference, and improve navigation. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified by email and via an in-app notice. Continued use of the Service after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related question, request, or grievance: support@smartbizindia.com.
